Professional Barista Training

Elevate your coffee skills with our comprehensive barista training program. Learn from certified coffee professionals and master the techniques that make exceptional espresso-based beverages.

What You'll Learn:
  • Espresso extraction techniques and timing
  • Milk steaming and latte art fundamentals
  • Coffee bean selection and grinding
  • Equipment maintenance and calibration
  • Customer service excellence
Duration: 4 hours intensive training
Group Size: Maximum 8 participants
Investment: $120 per person
Schedule: Saturdays 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM

Coffee Cupping Experience

Join our professional coffee cupping sessions to develop your palate and discover the intricate flavors and aromas that make each coffee unique. Perfect for coffee enthusiasts and professionals alike.

Experience Includes:
  • Guided tasting of 6 different single-origin coffees
  • Learn professional cupping protocols
  • Flavor wheel education and vocabulary
  • Understanding terroir and processing methods
  • Take-home cupping notes and coffee samples
Duration: 2 hours guided session
Group Size: Maximum 12 participants
Investment: $45 per person
Schedule: Fridays 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Hands-on Coffee Workshop

Immerse yourself in the world of specialty coffee with our interactive workshops. Learn various brewing methods and discover which techniques work best for different coffee beans and your personal taste preferences.

Workshop Highlights:
  • Pour-over techniques (V60, Chemex, Kalita)
  • French Press and Cold Brew methods
  • Understanding grind size and water temperature
  • Bean-to-cup ratio optimization
  • Home brewing equipment recommendations
Duration: 3 hours hands-on session
Group Size: Maximum 10 participants
Investment: $75 per person
Schedule: Sundays 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM
